MPETuner

Just intonation on keyboards in real time! Microtonal modulation! Strange and novel sonorities! Welcome to MPETuner!

MPETuner is an audio plugin which tunes MIDI data in just intonation in real time. Besides enabling beautiful justly-tuned chords on keyboards, this also enables many other cool things such as microtonal modulation, and unusual chord sonorities!

MPETuner was created in collaboration with, and while interning with Ben Bloomberg!
